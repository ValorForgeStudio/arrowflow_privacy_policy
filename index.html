Privacy Policy

Valor Forge Studio

✓ No data collected
Short version: Arrow Flow does not collect, store, or share any personal information. Your gameplay stays entirely on your device.

Information We Collect

Arrow Flow does not collect any personal information from its users. We do not require account registration, and we do not request access to any sensitive data on your device.

What We Do Not Do

✓ We do not collect your name, email address, or any personal identifiers.
✓ We do not track your location.
✓ We do not use analytics or crash-reporting tools that send data off your device.
✓ We do not display advertisements or use any ad networks.
✓ We do not share any data with third parties.

Data Storage

All game progress and settings (such as completed levels and preferences) are saved locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Third-Party Services

Arrow Flow does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or social login services. Your experience is entirely self-contained.

Children's Privacy

Because Arrow Flow collects no personal information whatsoever, it is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect data from children or any other users.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update Arrow Flow in a way that changes how data is handled, we will update this page and revise the effective date below. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
